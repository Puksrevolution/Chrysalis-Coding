Welcome to Chrysalis Coding!

At Chrysalis Coding, we provide a space where Women in Tech and advocates of this movement to detail their experiences of working within this industry. Enabling future generations with inspiration and learning to bring and adopt into their own experiences.

This site was designed to promote the positives and the negatives of working as a Woman in Tech, or as an onlooker. It is designed to work on the go, and we all have mobile smartphones at our disposal, meaning you can read or add to the discussions in the Forum at any time, with the touch of a button right from your own pocket! Together, we can further promote Women in Tech, and empower the future!
